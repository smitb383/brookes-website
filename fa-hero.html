Alchemists

Role
Project Founder, Director of Exhibition Production & Curation
A 150+ object exhibition and events program exploring hopeful futures for fashion — bringing together 50+ global designers at Gardens by the Bay, Singapore.
Location Gardens by the Bay, Singapore
Duration 11/2023 — 01/2026
AbOut

Fashion's Alchemists was a 150+ object exhibition and events program that I co-created exploring hopeful futures for fashion. The exhibition brought together 50+ global designers and brands working across material innovation, emerging technologies, and sustainable practices.

Developed in collaboration with Gardens by the Bay, Singapore's most visited cultural destination, and backed by the Singapore Government, the project was contracted as a 3-year, annually recurring 5-month exhibition accompanied by a gala, summit, and public events series.

My Role

01 — Creative Direction & Curation
I conceived of the project's concept & narrative. Over the 2+ years of the project's development, I led creative direction and curation.
  • Sourced, curated, and commissioned work from 50+ international artists including LVMH Prize winners and Met-featured designers
  • Conceived the curatorial framework across five thematic chapters and a 300+ page exhibition brief
  • Creative directed the branding, experience design, lighting, and visitor journey
  • Worked hand in hand with spatial and landscape designers to bring the vision to life
  • Commissioned and developed 20+ interactive installations with designers, technologists, and biologists
  • Authored the exhibition text, writing, decks, and creative briefs for designers and vendors
  • Directed all creative reviews across prototypes, content development, and spatial design
Curation Artist Management Exhibition Design Creative Briefs Spatial Design Experience Design
02 — Production
I led exhibition production across creative, technical, and operations – building the teams and systems required to turn experimental fashion into a feasible, accessible experience.
  • Led full production across creative, technical, and operations over a $3M budget
  • Managed vendor procurement, fabrication partners, AV, lighting, and creative technology vendors
  • Built production frameworks: briefs, schedules, run-of-show, and quality control systems
  • Led logistics strategy and cross-border shipping for 150+ objects from 50+ international artists
  • Coordinated compliance, permits, and government stakeholder approvals
Budget Management Vendor Procurement Logistics Fabrication Run-of-Show
03 — Founding & Strategy
I secured institutional backing and strategic partners with my co-founder, creating & delivering pitches and evolving the exhibition & events to align with the Singapore Government and Gardens by the Bay's priorities & KPIS.
  • Secured $3M in government-backed sponsorship as primary liaison to Gardens by the Bay and the Singapore Government
  • Created and pitched sponsorship decks to government bodies, corporate sponsors, and cultural institutions
  • Directed visual and graphic identity — branding, marketing collateral, press materials, and campaign assets
  • Supported production strategy and leadership decisions across summits, sponsor activations, gala events, and public programming
  • Developed partnerships with LaSalle College of the Arts and Singapore Fashion Council, including a design award and competition
